# Signals and Systems (EEE307)

[MIT Signals and Systems (6.003)](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/6-003-signals-and-systems-fall-2011)

Note: The textbook MIT uses, [Signals and Systems (Oppenheim)](https://annas-archive.gl/md5/8169478eeb6b45dc63f1165f8cb90681), was hard to read. And my suspisions were validated by a [reddit user](https://web.archive.org/web/20260324191608/https://redlib.catsarch.com/r/EngineeringStudents/comments/4kost0/comment/d3hf9qo/).

It could be that the MIT book is more of a standard reference than a self-study book.

This Oxford book is apparently better for self-studying: [Linear Systems and Signals (B.P. Lathi)](https://annas-archive.gl/md5/4e15e058430c801865a53d6d9126dd02)
